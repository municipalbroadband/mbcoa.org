About

Municipal Broadband Coalition of America (MBCoA) provides education, advocacy, and guidance to communities interested in building publicly-owned internet infrastructure. Municipal broadband is one of the best investments a community can make in their future. We are dedicated to bringing reliable, competitive broadband internet access to everyone in the United States.

MBCoA is a registered social welfare organization operating under IRC section 501(c)(4).
